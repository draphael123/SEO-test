FAQ

General education — not medical advice. A licensed clinician can help determine what's appropriate for you.

How do I know if TRT or HRT is right for me? +
A clinician considers symptoms, medical history, and (when relevant) labs together to determine whether treatment is appropriate.
Do I need labs? +
Many care plans involve labs for evaluation and/or ongoing monitoring. What's needed depends on your situation and clinician judgment.
How does follow-up and monitoring work? +
Follow-ups review progress, symptoms, and (when appropriate) lab markers so a clinician can make careful adjustments and support safety.
Is everything "all-inclusive"? +
Offerings vary by plan and by state law. This page is a general overview; specifics are confirmed during intake and clinician review.
Can you guarantee outcomes? +
No. Responses vary by person. Any medical decision should be individualized and guided by a licensed clinician.
